# Financial Data Sources Explainer

This document outlines the CSV datasets generated by the `financial_data_pull.ipynb` pipeline. It details the available headers, what the data represents, and provides the source links for future reference.

---

## 1. Bank of England (BoE) Base Rate
**File**: `output/boe_base_rate.csv`
**Source**: [Bank of England IADB](https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/boeapps/database/)

This represents the official central bank interest rate dating back to 1970.
* **`DATE`**: Date of the rate observation.
* **`IUDBEDR`**: The official UK Bank of England Base Rate (expressed as a percentage).

---

## 2. Bank of England (BoE) Quoted Household Rates
**File**: `output/boe_quoted_household_rates.csv`
**Source**: [Bank of England IADB](https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/boeapps/database/)

Aggregates average quoted interest rates for retail banking products in the UK (since 2010).
* **`DATE`**: End of month observation date.
* **`IUMTLMV`**: Average quoted household rate on a 2-year fixed rate mortgage at 75% LTV.
* **`IUMBV34`**: Average quoted household rate on a 5-year fixed rate mortgage at 75% LTV.
* **`IUM55L2`**: Average quoted household 1-year fixed rate bond.
* **`IUM5957`**: Average quoted rate on instant access deposit accounts.

---

## 3. HM Land Registry UK House Price Index (HPI)
**File**: `output/uk_hpi_data.csv`
**Source**: [HM Land Registry HPI Data](https://publicdata.landregistry.gov.uk/market-trend-data/house-price-index-data/)

Comprehensive monthly report on house prices across the UK by region and property type.
* **`Date`**: The month of the index release.
* **`RegionName`**: Geographic region (e.g., "Yorkshire and The Humber", "London").
* **`AreaCode`**: The standardized ONS area code.
* **`AveragePrice`**: The average property price in GBP.
* **`Index`**: The House Price Index value.
* **`1m%Change` & `12m%Change`**: The monthly and annual percentage change in price.
* **Property Type Breakdowns**: Individual price, index, and volume metrics for Detached (`DetachedPrice`), Semi-Detached, Terraced, and Flats.
* **Buyer Types**: Breaks down metrics for Cash (`CashPrice`), Mortgage (`MortgagePrice`), and First Time Buyers (`FTBPrice`).
* **`SalesVolume`**: Number of transactions recorded.

---

## 4. ONS UK Inflation (CPI)
**File**: `output/ons_cpi_inflation.csv`
**Source**: [Office for National Statistics (ONS) D7G7](https://www.ons.gov.uk/economy/inflationandpriceindices/timeseries/d7g7/mm23)

Contains the official UK Consumer Price Index (Annual Rate 00: All Items, 2015=100).
*Note: Because the raw data writes straight to disk, the CSV contains 8 rows of ONS metadata before the tabular data begins.*
* **Row 9 onwards (Column 1 / `Date`)**: Year and Month (e.g., "2025 JAN").
* **Row 9 onwards (Column 2 / `CPI_Rate`)**: The measured CPI annual inflation rate.

---

## 5. UK Open Banking: Personal Current Accounts
**File**: `output/uk_open_banking_pca.csv`
**Source**: Mandated CMA9 Open Banking unauthenticated REST APIs (e.g., NatWest, Lloyds, Barclays)

Dynamically aggregated retail product offerings. Very useful for comparative market analysis in wealth management.
* **`Bank`**: The parent banking group (e.g., Lloyds, NatWest).
* **`Brand`**: The specific trading brand.
* **`Product Name`**: The marketing name of the bank account.
* **`Segment`**: Target audience segment (e.g., "Basic", "General", "Premier").

---

## 6. FCA Mortgage Lending Statistics (MLAR)
**File**: `output/fca_mlar_summary.csv`
**Source**: [FCA MLAR Long-run Statistics](https://www.fca.org.uk/publication/data/mlar-statistics-summary-long-run.xlsx)

The high-level macro "Summary 1" sheet extracted directly from the FCA's long-run Excel dataset. Due to the complex formatting of the source Excel file, this CSV retains a multi-hierarchical layout, requiring location-based parsing depending on the metric needed.
* **`Unnamed: 0`**: Contains the row-level metric descriptions (e.g., "Gross advances", "Balances outstanding").
* **`Unnamed: 1` through `Unnamed: 79`**: Represent the quarterly time-series values across time (each column corresponds to a fiscal quarter). 

---

## 7. Companies House Corporate Demographics
**File**: `output/companies_house_sample.csv`
**Source**: [Companies House Free Data Product](http://download.companieshouse.gov.uk/en_output.html)

A representative 50,000 row cross-sectional sample of active UK corporate entities. Includes 55 total columns.
* **`CompanyName`**: Registered legal name of the entity.
* **`CompanyNumber`**: Unique official registration ID.
* **`RegAddress.*`**: 9 columns detailing the registered office address (e.g., `RegAddress.PostCode`, `RegAddress.Town`).
* **`CompanyCategory`**: E.g., "Private Limited Company".
* **`CompanyStatus`**: E.g., "Active".
* **`IncorporationDate`**: E.g., "19/07/2012".
* **`SICCode.SicText_1` - `SICCode.SicText_4`**: The Standard Industrial Classification indicating the market sector of the business.
* **`Mortgages.*`**: Number of active/satisfied charges against the company.
* **`PreviousName_*`**: Historical legal names.

---

## 8. FRED US Mortgage Rates (Reference)
**File**: `output/fred_30y_mortgage.csv`
**Source**: [Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED)](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/MORTGAGE30US)

US 30-Year Fixed Rate Mortgage Average, used strictly as an international macro reference point.
* **`DATE`**: Weekly observation date.
* **`MORTGAGE30US`**: The average 30-year fixed mortgage rate across the United States.
